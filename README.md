# Twisted Literature

Twisted Literature is a platform dedicated to providing a space for creating and experiencing intricate and thought-provoking short stories.

## Description

- **What**: Twisted Literature offers immersive and captivating short stories.
- **Who**: It's for storytellers and readers seeking captivating short narratives.
- **Why**: We aim to foster a community of individuals who share a passion for storytelling and want to explore the boundless realms of imagination through the written word.

## How to Use

1. Visit the website.
2. Sign up for an account if you're a storyteller or start reading if you're a reader.
3. Start creating or enjoying captivating short stories.

## Contributions

We welcome contributions from writers and readers who want to share their stories or improve the platform. Feel free to contribute by submitting your own stories or suggesting enhancements.

## Contact

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can reach out to us at contact@twistedliterature.com.

We hope you enjoy your time at Twisted Literature!
